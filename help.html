How To Use Shift-X

1. Add Workers

Open Workers and add your staff members.

2. Add Credentials

Store worker qualifications and expiry dates.

3. Monitor Compliance

Dashboard shows expiring and expired credentials.

4. Create Shifts

Create shifts and assign workers.

5. Broadcast Shifts

Tick "Broadcast To Network" to share shifts with other providers.

6. View Shared Shifts

Open Shared Shifts to see shifts shared by other providers.
